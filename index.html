William Shakespeare

English novelist famous for her keen social commentary and romantic fiction, including "Pride and Prejudice."

Ernest Hemingway

American author and journalist, celebrated for his concise prose style in novels like "The Old Man and the Sea."

J.K. Rowling

British author best known for creating the "Harry Potter" series, which has captivated readers worldwide.

Mark Twain

American writer and humorist, famous for classics like "The Adventures of Tom Sawyer" and "Adventures of Huckleberry Finn."
